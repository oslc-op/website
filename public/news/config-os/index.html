OSLC Configuration Management Version 1.0 published as an OASIS Standard

Jul 23, 2023
Andrew Berezovskyi
No comments

OASIS is pleased to announce that the OSLC Configuration Management Version 1.0 has been published after reaching the OASIS Standard status.

OASIS congratulates the members of the OSLC Open Project and the community of implementers, developers and users who have brought the work successfully to this milestone.
